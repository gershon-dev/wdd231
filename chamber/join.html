NP Membership Benefits

  • ✅ Chamber membership listing in the online directory
  • ✅ Access to monthly networking events (subsidised rate)
  • ✅ Chamber newsletter subscription
  • ✅ Access to shared meeting spaces (limited)
  • ✅ Certificate of Chamber membership

Exclusively for registered non-profit organisations. Proof of non-profit status required.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • ✅ All NP Membership benefits
  • ✅ Business profile in the online member directory
  • ✅ Access to Chamber-organised trade workshops
  • ✅ 10% discount on Chamber event registration
  • ✅ Access to Chamber's legal and regulatory advice helpline
  • ✅ Monthly business digest newsletter

Annual fee: GHS 500. Renewable each year.

Silver Membership Benefits

  • ✅ All Bronze Membership benefits
  • ✅ Featured listing in the online member directory
  • ✅ Spotlight eligibility on the Chamber homepage
  • ✅ 20% discount on all Chamber events
  • ✅ One free booth at the annual Ghana Business Expo
  • ✅ Access to Chamber's SME finance advisory sessions
  • ✅ Social media mentions and Chamber blog features

Annual fee: GHS 1,200. Renewable each year.

Gold Membership Benefits

  • ⭐ All Silver Membership benefits
  • ⭐ Premium spotlight on homepage — guaranteed rotation
  • ⭐ Priority listing at the top of the member directory
  • ⭐ Free attendance at all Chamber events (up to 3 staff)
  • ⭐ Dedicated Chamber account manager
  • ⭐ Speaking opportunities at Chamber-hosted forums
  • ⭐ Co-branding rights on Chamber publications and events
  • ⭐ Annual recognition award eligibility

Annual fee: GHS 2,500. Includes priority renewal.
